About Kamen Rider W

In order to maintain the peace of Windy City, the private detective ‧ Hidari Shotaro and
his knowledgeable companion ‧ Philip together transformed into Kamen Rider W, and fought against the monster named Dopant that appeared one after another.





Kamen Rider W's Characteristics


    Kamen Rider W is transformed by two people.

    Kamen Rider w can change different forms by switching transformation items.

    The color scheme of Kamen Rider w is boldly designed into two colors separated in half. As he changes form, the colors on both sides of his body will also change.
