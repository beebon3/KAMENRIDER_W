Complete Selection Modification

release a redesign of an already released DX toy, usually that of a Kamen Rider's transformation devices. These new releases have much more detail than the previous DX toy, as they are meant to be very close to the show prop. These toys also have show-accurate functionality i.e. they function just like how the actual device would do so in-show.

Double Driver

Kamen Rider Double's transformation belt, a second generation Memory Driver developed for the user to link with Philip to access his abilities, and the successor of the Lost Driver in terms of belt design. Unlike earlier models, the Double Driver uses two specialized Gaia Memories at once for even more power for the user.

Faiz Gear

The Faiz Gear was the second set of Kamen Rider Gear developed by the Smart Brain Corporation to protect the Orphnoch King. Like most of the Rider Gears that Smart Brain developed, the Faiz Gear can only be worn by Orphnochs or humans implanted with Orphnoch DNA.

Henshin Belt Arcle

Kuuga's transformation belt. An ancient belt created by the Lynt Tribe, it is capable of strengthening it's wearer and create a biological armor for them to use in combat, but is only usable to those with a righteous heart to prevent abuse of the belt's power.
